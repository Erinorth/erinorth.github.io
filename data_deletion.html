Data Deletion

Instructions for deleting data inside the MealRecovery app.

Delete a single day log

  1. Open the MealRecovery app.
  2. Go to the Log tab.
  3. In the "Logged today" card, tap Delete today log.

This deletes only today's log.

Delete all logs

  1. Open the MealRecovery app.
  2. Go to the History tab.
  3. Scroll to Data tools.
  4. Tap Clear all logs and confirm.

This removes all stored meal timing logs and resets your streak history.

Guest mode note

Guest mode has no account data on the server. Clearing logs in the app removes local data.
