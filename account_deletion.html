# MealRecovery - Account Deletion Request ## How to Delete Your Account To delete your MealRecovery account and all associated data, please follow these steps: 1. Open the MealRecovery app 2. Go to Settings > Account 3. Tap "Delete Account" 4. Confirm deletion **OR** Email us at: support@yourdomain.com with subject "Delete MealRecovery Account" ## What Data Will Be Deleted When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed: - Email address - Workout logs and meal timing records - Streak data - User preferences - Subscription information ## Data Retention - Data will be deleted within 30 days of your request - Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., payment records for tax purposes) For questions, contact: support@yourdomain.com
