Account Deletion

Instructions for deleting your account inside the MealRecovery app.

In-app account deletion steps

  1. Open the MealRecovery app.
  2. Tap the Account icon in the top-right corner.
  3. On the Account screen, tap Request account deletion.
  4. Your email app will open with a prefilled deletion request.
  5. Send the email to north.affhub@gmail.com.

üìå If the email app cannot open, the support email will be copied so you can paste it manually.
